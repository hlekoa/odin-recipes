Holiday ONLY Mashed Potatoes

  • Home

Description

These mashed potatoes are too fattening to have on a regular basis. I only make these on holidays, and everyone wonders what's in them that's makes them so rich...r

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make the best holiday only mashed potatoes:

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

You'll need to follow these steps to achieve a perfect recipe below:

  1. Step 1: Place potatoes in a large pot with enough water to cover.
  2. Step 2: Bring to a boil, and cook until fork tender, about 20 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Drain, add butter, and mash using a potato masher, ricer or mixer.
  4. Step 4: Gradully beat in the heavy cream. Serve hot!
